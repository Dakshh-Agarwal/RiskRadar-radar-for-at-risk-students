# About RiskRadar

## Our Mission

RiskRadar is an intelligent student success platform that uses advanced machine learning to help educational institutions identify at-risk students early and provide timely interventions.

## What We Do

- **Early Detection**: Predict student dropout rates and academic performance with high accuracy
- **Actionable Insights**: Provide educators with clear, data-driven recommendations
- **Prevent Dropouts**: Identify struggling students before they disengage
- **Data-Driven Support**: Optimize student support strategies through analytics

## Why RiskRadar?

Education institutions face significant challenges with student retention. RiskRadar bridges the gap between data and action, empowering educators with the tools they need to make a real difference in students' lives.

## Key Features

✓ Real-time Student Risk Assessment  
✓ Predictive Analytics Dashboard  
✓ Easy-to-use Student Data Forms  
✓ Secure Authentication & Data Protection  
✓ Comprehensive Reporting & Insights

## Technology Stack

- **Backend**: FastAPI, Python, Machine Learning (Scikit-learn)
- **Frontend**: React, Redux, Tailwind CSS
- **Database**: Integration-ready architecture

## Our Vision

Transform education through data-driven insights, ensuring every student has the support they need to succeed.
